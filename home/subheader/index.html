SUPPORTING FC BAYERN

We are an FC Bayern Munich supporter’s group based in Los Angeles, California USA. We get together for Bundesliga games, DFB Pokal games, Champions League games, and German National Team games! We also hold special events like Stammtisch, holiday parties, and organize trips to Munich to see FC Bayern Munich play at the Allianz Arena in Germany.

Stay tuned as we announce things like fundraisers, special events, and news. Make sure to follow us on social media! You can find us on Instagram and Twitter.
